Who We Are

Where Design Meets
Purpose

We are a multidisciplinary design and planning studio based in Chennai, specialising in spaces that serve both beauty and function. From crafting breathtaking interiors to engineering productive farm ecosystems — every space has a story waiting to be told.

Our work spans residential interiors, commercial spaces, agricultural planning, and sustainable farming systems. We bring the same precision and passion to a 3D interior visualisation as we do to a polyhouse irrigation plan.
