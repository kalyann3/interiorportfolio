About Us

Spaces Built with
Intention

We are a multidisciplinary design and planning studio based in Chennai, where architectural thinking meets agricultural innovation. Our work spans two worlds — the refined aesthetic of interior design and the productive science of sustainable farming systems.

Founded with a simple belief: every space, whether a living room or a polyhouse, deserves the same level of care, creativity, and technical expertise.

From 2D floor plans and photorealistic 3D renders to hydroponics systems and goat farm infrastructure — we are your end-to-end design and planning partner.
